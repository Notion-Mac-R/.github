## Overview

Notion Advanced is an all-in-one workspace that replaces the scattered collection of tools most teams rely on with a single, flexible environment for writing, planning, and collaboration. Documents, databases, wikis, and task boards all live together in one place.

Pages are infinitely nestable and fully customizable. Start with a blank page or choose from a library of templates, then build exactly the structure your project needs using blocks of text, media, tables, and more.

Databases are the heart of Notion's power. Create a table, switch it to a kanban board, a calendar, or a gallery — the same data, viewed the way that makes sense for the task at hand.

Collaboration is built in from the ground up. Share pages, assign tasks, leave comments, and mention teammates inside the same documents where the work actually happens.

Notion Advanced is the workspace for teams and individuals who want one tool that truly does it all, without compromise or endless app switching between notes, tasks, wikis, and project boards.

## Performance

Notion balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management reduces overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance compared to software-only solutions. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
